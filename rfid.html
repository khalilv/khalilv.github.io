    This RFID touchless checkout system was the flagship product of Dynamic Integration Technologies, a startup I founded with a close friend Shakil Jiwa. It started in the summer of 2020 when both of us had internships cancelled because of COVID-19. Shakil was introduced to UHF RFID technology from a collegue and in particular that it could be used to uniquely identify tagged items without direct line-of-sight and read up to 1000 items per second. Having almost no background in the field, this was fascinating to us. After some initial experiments, and seeing the increasing need for touchless technology, we had an idea to build a touchless checkout and inventory management system using UHF RFID technology.
